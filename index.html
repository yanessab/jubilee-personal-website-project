A little bit about me.

I’ve made my home base in NYC with my husband. My journey into tech started with a personal blog. As I started sharing our adventures online, I felt frustrated with how little I could do to manipulate the blog plus also having to wait for my husband to help me. Fast forward 7 years, a forgotten blog plus the need to expand my job options. I finally took the jump and signed up for a Web Development class.

I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs. Once I built my very first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom and the versatility I was looking for in my life and work. Now I’ve made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer’s PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
